# Support policy

Database Accelerator is a personal experimental project. It is provided without support, warranty, service-level agreement, maintenance promise, or compatibility guarantee.

The maintainer may answer questions or investigate reports when interested and available. Silence does not mean a report was accepted, scheduled, or reproduced. Feature requests and roadmap votes do not control project direction.

Use the documentation, source, tests, and versioned plans as the primary support material. If repository Issues or Discussions are enabled, they are an optional communication channel, not a help desk.

For a guaranteed response, deployment review, operational help, or production assurance, use a different supported product or engage your own qualified engineer. Security vulnerabilities must follow `SECURITY.md`.
